Frequently Asked Questions — Exterior House Cleaning in Melbourne

How often should I get my house exterior cleaned in Melbourne?

Most Melbourne homes benefit from an exterior wash every one to two years. Properties near busy roads accumulate pollution grime more quickly, and homes with rendered or painted walls are particularly prone to mould and algae growth in Melbourne's variable climate. A regular clean prevents long-term staining and protects exterior surfaces from biological damage.

Will pressure washing damage my rendered walls?

High-pressure washing can crack, chip or erode render if applied incorrectly. For rendered walls, we use a soft-wash technique — lower pressure combined with appropriate cleaning solutions — which effectively removes mould, algae and staining without damaging the surface. This approach is safe for all types of render including acrylic, cement and texture coat.

Can you clean a house before it's painted?

Yes — exterior cleaning before repainting is highly recommended and will extend the life of your new paint job significantly. Painting over dirty, mouldy or chalky surfaces leads to poor adhesion and premature peeling. We can clean your home's exterior to a standard that's ready for the painter, saving you time and ensuring better long-term results.

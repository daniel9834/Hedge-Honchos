Frequently Asked Questions — Pre-Inspection Cleaning in Melbourne

What are tenants responsible for regarding the garden at inspection time?

In Victoria, tenants are generally responsible for maintaining the garden in the same condition as when they moved in, taking into account fair wear and tear. This typically includes mowing the lawn, keeping garden beds weeded and removing excess leaf litter. If a tenant has allowed the garden to significantly deteriorate, the cost of restoration may be claimed from the bond.

How quickly can you attend a pre-inspection clean-up?

In most cases we can schedule a pre-inspection clean-up within two to four business days. For urgent situations — such as a snap inspection notice — contact us directly and we will do our best to accommodate your timeline. We service Melbourne's north and east suburbs from Viewbank and can often respond quickly to nearby properties.

Do you provide before-and-after photos for property managers?

Yes — we photograph the property before and after every pre-inspection clean-up and provide these images to you on the same day. These records are valuable evidence for property managers, particularly when assessing tenant responsibility for garden condition and supporting any bond claim for restoration costs.

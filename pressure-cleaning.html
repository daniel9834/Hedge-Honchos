Frequently Asked Questions — Pressure Cleaning in Melbourne

How much does pressure cleaning a driveway cost in Melbourne?

Driveway pressure cleaning in Melbourne typically ranges from $100 to $300 depending on the size, surface type and level of staining. Brick paving takes more time than plain concrete, and heavily stained surfaces may need a second pass or pre-treatment. We provide a free, fixed-price quote before starting so there are no surprises.

How often should I pressure clean my driveway or patio?

Annual pressure cleaning is recommended for most Melbourne driveways and outdoor paved areas to remove the buildup of dirt, algae and moss that accumulates through Melbourne's wet winters. Areas in shade or under trees may need cleaning more frequently. A regular clean also makes surfaces safer by removing slippery algae and moss growth.

Can pressure cleaning remove oil stains from a concrete driveway?

Pressure cleaning alone can lift surface oil but deeply set oil stains in porous concrete may require a pre-treatment with a degreaser or poultice product before pressure washing. We assess the stain type and age before starting, apply pre-treatment where needed, and pressure wash to achieve the best possible result. Results vary depending on how long the stain has been present.

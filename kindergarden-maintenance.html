Frequently Asked Questions — Kindergarden Maintenance in Melbourne

What chemicals do you use in child-care garden settings?

In kindergarden and childcare environments, we default to hand weeding and non-toxic methods wherever possible. If herbicide is required for pathways or areas inaccessible to children, we use registered products applied at correct label rates, with full re-entry times observed. We always communicate with the centre director before any chemical application and can provide product data sheets on request.

Can you work at the kindergarden during operating hours?

Routine mowing and garden tidying can often be done early in the morning before children arrive, or during outdoor play-free times. We work with your centre to identify safe windows for garden maintenance. Tasks involving power tools, heavy equipment or chemical application are always scheduled outside operating hours to eliminate any risk to children.

What safety checks or credentials do your crew hold?

All Hedge Honchos crew members working on childcare and kindergarden sites hold current public liability insurance. We can provide a certificate of currency and relevant documentation for your centre's compliance records. Requirements for Working With Children checks vary depending on whether staff have direct contact with children, and we are happy to discuss your centre's specific requirements.

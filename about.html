Our Story

Built on a love for outdoor spaces

Hedge Honchos started with a straightforward belief: your garden and outdoor space deserves the same level of care and craftsmanship you put into your home. Founder Leon built the business from the ground up, combining hands-on expertise with genuine passion for every job.

What began as a one-person operation has grown into a trusted Melbourne team, taking on everything from weekly lawn mowing to complex tree removals and full garden transformations. We bring the same care and attention to every job, no matter the size.

We work with homeowners, property managers, body corporates and commercial clients across Melbourne and surrounding suburbs. Our approach is always the same: show up on time, do the job properly, and leave your property looking better than we found it.

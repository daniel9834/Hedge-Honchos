Frequently Asked Questions — Tree Removal in Melbourne

Do I need a permit to remove a tree in Melbourne?

In most Melbourne councils, you need a permit to remove a tree above a certain height or trunk diameter, and protected or significant trees almost always require council approval regardless of size. Requirements vary significantly between councils, so always check with us or your local council before proceeding. Removing a protected tree without a permit can result in substantial fines.

How much does tree removal cost in Melbourne?

Tree removal pricing depends on the tree's height, trunk diameter, access to the site and whether stump grinding is included. Small trees might cost from $300 to $500, while large or complex removals can cost significantly more. We provide a free on-site quote so you know exactly what you're up for before any work begins.

What happens to the wood and debris after the tree is removed?

We remove all timber, branches and green waste from your property as part of the standard service, leaving the site clean. If you'd like to keep some firewood, let us know in advance and we can cut and stack sections for you. Stump grinding can also be added to complete the job.

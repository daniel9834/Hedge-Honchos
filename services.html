Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer regular maintenance schedules?

Yes. Most of our clients book us for regular weekly, fortnightly or monthly visits. We'll recommend a schedule based on your garden's size and needs, and you'll always have the same team on your property.

Are you certified and insured?

Absolutely. Our team holds all relevant certifications and carries comprehensive public liability insurance. We're also certified arborists for all tree work, which is important for council compliance and safety.

Do I need a council permit for tree removal?

It depends on the tree, its size, and your local council's regulations. Many significant trees are protected. We'll advise you on permit requirements and can prepare the necessary arborist report if needed.

How do I get a quote?

The easiest way is to fill in our quote form with a description and photos of your space. We'll get back to you promptly with a no-obligation price. For urgent or complex jobs, give us a call directly.

Do you service my suburb?

We cover Melbourne and a wide range of surrounding suburbs. Check our Areas We Service page for the full list, or just ask us — we'll let you know if we can reach you.

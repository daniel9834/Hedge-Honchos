Frequently Asked Questions — Commercial Property Maintenance in Melbourne

Can you maintain commercial properties while tenants are on site?

Yes. We schedule visits to minimise disruption to your tenants and can work around operating hours, including early morning starts and weekend visits where required. For high-traffic commercial properties, we plan and sequence the work to ensure pedestrian access and safety is maintained throughout the service.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance contracts for commercial properties in Melbourne?

Yes — ongoing contracts are a core part of our commercial service. We offer fortnightly, monthly or custom-scheduled contracts with locked-in pricing, service records and direct communication with your property manager. Contracts provide consistency, accountability and priority scheduling compared to ad-hoc bookings.

What level of public liability insurance do you carry?

Hedge Honchos carries comprehensive public liability insurance. We can provide a certificate of currency on request, which is required by many commercial landlords and property managers before works commence on site. Our insurance covers all work carried out across Melbourne's north and east.

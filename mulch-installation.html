Frequently Asked Questions — Mulch Installation in Melbourne

What type of mulch is best for Melbourne gardens?

Bark and woodchip mulch are the most popular choices for Melbourne gardens, providing excellent moisture retention and weed suppression while breaking down to improve soil health over time. Pebble or crushed rock mulch is a low-maintenance option suited to contemporary or water-wise garden styles. The best choice depends on your plants, aesthetic preferences and budget.

How deep should mulch be applied?

For effective weed suppression and moisture retention, we recommend applying organic mulch at a depth of 75 to 100mm. Too shallow and weeds push through easily; too deep can affect soil oxygen levels and plant roots. We calculate the correct volume for your garden area and ensure even application at the right depth throughout.

How often should I top up mulch in my Melbourne garden?

Organic mulch breaks down over time and typically needs topping up every one to two years, depending on the type of mulch and conditions in your garden. We can set up seasonal top-up reminders and revisit annually to keep mulch at an effective depth. A light refresh in autumn is particularly beneficial to protect plant roots through winter.

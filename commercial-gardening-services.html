Frequently Asked Questions — Commercial Gardening Services in Melbourne

What types of commercial businesses do you service in Melbourne?

We work with a wide range of commercial clients across Melbourne's north and east, including office buildings, retail centres, industrial properties, medical and allied health facilities, hospitality venues, and mixed-use developments. Whether you need a simple monthly mow or a comprehensive grounds management program, we can tailor a service to suit your site.

Can you help redesign and replant an outdated commercial garden?

Yes. We assist with garden improvements ranging from refreshing tired garden beds with new mulch and seasonal colour plants through to more substantial replanting schemes. We select species appropriate for your site conditions, maintenance budget and aesthetic requirements, and can stage the work to suit your operational needs and budget timeline.

Do you provide a single point of contact for commercial clients?

Yes. Commercial clients are assigned a dedicated contact who manages their account, responds to queries and coordinates all scheduled and ad-hoc work. This means you're not dealing with a call centre or a different crew member each time — you have consistent, accountable service from a team that knows your property.

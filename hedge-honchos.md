# Hedge Honchos

## Business Description

Hedge Honchos offers expert, full-service outdoor property maintenance, specializing in both meticulous gardening and safe, efficient tree removal. From precise hedging, lawn care, and planting to complex tree felling, stump grinding, and emergency storm work, our certified team ensures your landscape is both beautiful and safe. We serve residential and commercial clients, dedicated to enhancing your property's aesthetics and health with professional care and reliability.

Frequently Asked Questions — Gutter Cleaning in Melbourne

How often should I get my gutters cleaned in Melbourne?

Most Melbourne homes should have their gutters cleaned at least twice a year — once in late autumn after the leaves have fallen and again in late spring before the storm season. Properties near large deciduous trees or with overhanging eucalypts may need cleaning three to four times per year, as heavy leaf fall can block gutters rapidly.

What happens if I don't clean my gutters?

Blocked gutters cause water to back up and overflow, leading to damage to fascias, soffits and exterior walls. Over time, sustained moisture can cause roof timber rot, ceiling leaks and even foundation issues where water pools around the base of the house. Regular gutter cleaning is one of the most cost-effective forms of home maintenance.

Do you clean gutters on double-storey homes in Melbourne?

Yes. We safely access gutters on single and double-storey homes using appropriate ladders and equipment. All work at height is carried out in accordance with safety requirements, and our team is fully insured for work on residential properties of all heights across Melbourne's north and east.
